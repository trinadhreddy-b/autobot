ACME Software — Customer Support FAQ
=====================================

PRODUCT OVERVIEW
----------------
ACME Software is a cloud-based project management tool that helps teams
collaborate, track tasks, and deliver projects on time.

Current version: 4.2.1
Release date: January 2025

PRICING & PLANS
---------------
Free Plan:
- Up to 3 projects
- Up to 5 team members
- 1 GB storage
- Email support

Pro Plan — $12/user/month:
- Unlimited projects
- Unlimited team members
- 50 GB storage
- Priority email & chat support
- Advanced reporting

Enterprise Plan — Custom pricing:
- Everything in Pro
- SSO / SAML authentication
- Dedicated account manager
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- On-premise deployment option

GETTING STARTED
---------------
1. Sign up at https://acme-software.example.com/signup
2. Verify your email address
3. Create your first project using the "+ New Project" button
4. Invite team members via Settings → Team → Invite Members
5. Download the mobile app (iOS and Android available)

ACCOUNT & BILLING
-----------------
Q: How do I upgrade my plan?
A: Go to Settings → Billing → Upgrade Plan. Changes take effect immediately
   and you are only charged the prorated amount.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Billing → Cancel Subscription. Your account
   remains active until the end of the current billing period.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal.
   Invoicing is available for Enterprise customers.

Q: How do I get a refund?
A: Refunds are available within 14 days of charge. Contact
   billing@acme-software.example.com with your order ID.

FEATURES & USAGE
----------------
Q: How do I create a task?
A: Inside any project, click "+ Add Task", fill in the title,
   assign a team member, set a due date, and click Save.

Q: Can I set recurring tasks?
A: Yes. When creating or editing a task, click "Repeat" and choose
   daily, weekly, monthly, or custom frequency.

Q: How do I use the Gantt chart view?
A: Open a project and click the "Gantt" tab in the top navigation.
   The Gantt chart is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

Q: Is there a time tracking feature?
A: Yes. Click the timer icon on any task to start tracking time.
   Reports are available under Analytics → Time Tracking.

Q: How do I integrate with Slack?
A: Go to Settings → Integrations → Slack, click "Connect", and
   authorise the ACME Slack app. You'll receive notifications for
   task assignments and due date reminders.

SECURITY & PRIVACY
------------------
Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Data is stored on AWS servers in the US-East region by default.
   EU data residency is available on the Enterprise plan.

Q: Is data encrypted?
A: Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).

Q: Does ACME comply with GDPR?
A: Yes. We are fully GDPR compliant. You can export or delete all your
   data at any time from Settings → Privacy → Data Management.

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
Q: I can't log in to my account.
A: Try resetting your password at the login page. If the issue persists,
   clear your browser cache or try a different browser. Contact support
   if you still can't access your account.

Q: The app is loading slowly.
A: Check your internet connection. If the issue persists, visit our
   status page to see if there is an ongoing incident.

Q: I accidentally deleted a project.
A: Deleted projects are recoverable within 30 days.
   Go to Settings → Trash → Restore Project.

Q: Can I import data from other tools?
A: Yes. We support CSV import and direct imports from Trello, Asana,
   and Monday.com. Go to Settings → Import/Export to get started.
